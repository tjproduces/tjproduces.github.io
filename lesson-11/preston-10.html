Preston: Your Favorite Plain

Preston Idaho is a unique town. Being completely surrounded by large mountains wherever you look, it's no wonder that people who pass through will always remember the town's name.

Although Preston is small in size, it packs a punch of interest with every acre within its bounds. For lovers of the outdoors, make sure to stop by the Glendale Reservoir and catch yourself some bass and trout!
