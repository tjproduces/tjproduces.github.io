The Short, Rich History of Soda Springs

Soda Springs is known as the "Oregon Trail Oasis" as the Pioneers were able to enjoy an abundance of water from springs when traveling through the area. Some would say that what is now our little town, was an answer to the prayers of many.

Soda Springs is also home to the world's only captive Geyser. This Geyser was found back in 1937, when settlers were drilling a hot water well for a bath house.
