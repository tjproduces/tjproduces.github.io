It's Time For an Adventure!

Down in the southeast corner of Idaho, only 2.5 miles north of the Utah Border, is where we find the community of Fish Haven.

Although small, Fish Haven isn't a community you want to pass by if you have the time! Home of Bear Lake State Park and the Minnetonka Cave, Fish Haven will have you embracing your inner adventurer in no time.
