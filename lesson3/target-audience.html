Target Audience

GoWeatherBoy will be designed with a simple, minimalistic approach at a user's first glance. If the user wants more detailed information, they can choose to dive deeper. This being said, the target audience will be people who want the most basic (but important) information presented without fluff. I can't think of a more proper term for it, but basically people who aren't weather geeks.


Personas

Meet Donnie, The Traveling Entrepreneur

25 Years Young | Traveler | More than likely into dropshipping

Donnie prefers GoWeatherBoy because of its minimalistic, sleek, modern design. Sometimes he gets curious about that thing called "Precipitation" because it makes him feel like a genius to know about it, but not often enough to warrant throwing that info at him right away.

Meet Emily, The Business Major

21 Years Young | Student | Hangs with the gals every weekend, because you can do that if you study business.

Why does Emily prefer GoWeatherBoy? Because all she needs to know is if the sun will be out next weekend when her and her girlfriends go hit the dunes.


Scenarios

I like to use my phone more than my computer, it's quicker.
